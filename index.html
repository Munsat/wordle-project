How to play:

Guess the word in 6 chances.

  1. Each guess has to be a valid 5 letter word.
  2. The colour of the tiles will change depending on how close your guess was to the word.
  3. W
    = The letter is in the word and in the correct spot
  4. W
    = The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot
  5. W
    = The letter is not in the word at all
